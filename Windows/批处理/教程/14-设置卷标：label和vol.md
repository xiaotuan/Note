### 设置卷标：label 和 vol

+ `vol` ：显示卷标
+ `label` ：显示卷标，同时提示输入新卷标
+ `label c:system` ：设置 C 盘的卷标为 system

```
d:\>vol
 Volume in drive D is SanDisk
 Volume Serial Number is 5F66-CD7B

d:\>label
Volume in drive D: is SanDisk
Volume Serial Number is 5F66-CD7B
Volume label (11 characters, ENTER for none)? Xiaotuan

d:\>vol
 Volume in drive D is Xiaotuan
 Volume Serial Number is 5F66-CD7B
```
